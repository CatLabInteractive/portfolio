Bazillionaire (2012 - concept)

Bazillionaire was a game concept / experiment with 3d webgl and websockets. The goal was to create a space trade simulator where thousands of players would drive a virtual economy. Using visual programming tools we would allow players to automate their factories and an auto-enforcable contract system would allow players to work for other players. The project was cancelled due to funding issues.

  • Space trade simulator
  • Budget: lots of time
  • Funding: subsidy was requested but denied (VAF aanvraag included in files)
  • Status: cancelled.

After the cold (2015 - concept)

After the cold was meant to be mashup of Route to Destiny and Dolumar. The game would be set in a post apocalyptic wasteland where clans fight for whatever is left. Our goal was to take the Route to Destiny setting and turn it into a turn based strategy game.

  • Turn based strategy
  • Budget: prototype estimated at €21.250
  • Funding: subsidy was requested but denied (VAF aanvraag included in files)
  • Status: frozen (get it?). A very early Gamejam Prototype is online
